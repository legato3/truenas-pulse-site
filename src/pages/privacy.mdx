# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-02_

TrueNAS Pulse does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What the app does

TrueNAS Pulse communicates **exclusively** with TrueNAS servers that you
configure. There is no developer-operated cloud service. The app contains
no analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party SDKs.

## Data not collected

- No analytics or tracking SDKs
- No crash reporting services
- No advertising identifiers
- No data shared with third parties
- No network requests except direct communication with the TrueNAS servers
  you configure

## Where your data lives

- **API tokens** are stored in the iOS Keychain on your device.
- **Server metadata** (URLs, names, ordering) is stored locally using
  `UserDefaults` on your device.
- **Local diagnostics**, when present, stay on the device and are never
  uploaded.
- If you enable **iCloud Sync** (Pro), your monitor configurations and server
  list sync across your devices via Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store. API
  tokens never leave the Keychain — iCloud Sync does not transport
  credentials.

## App Store privacy details

For App Store Connect's "Data Not Collected" disclosure: TrueNAS Pulse
collects no data from users, links no data to identity, and does no tracking.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the change will be summarised here with a new
"last updated" date. The app itself does not have the means to notify you of
a change because there is no telemetry channel — please check this page when
updating to a new version if you want to review what (if anything) changed.
